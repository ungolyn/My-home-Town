Visit Farin Ruwa Fall

Enjoy a beautiful adventure and sight of an amazing water fall, From the bus ride, to the bike ride, crossing 3 streams and the hike before seeing the waterfall that drops a total height of about 150 metres (492 ft). Located in Wamba, Nasarawa state. Nigeria

Top three activities to do at the water fall

Bike ride down to the water fall

This is quite an interesting part. You may have to walk a few meters to continue on the bike when you get to the streams.The bike experience takes around 30 minutes to the waterfall.

Hike up to top of the water fall for a magnificent view

When you arrive at the waterfall gate, you will take a walk to the fall itself which is about 150 meters from the gate.

Sit and enjoy the cool breeze beside the water fall

A gentle and cool breeze blows across and gradually reveals the magnificent waterfall which is about the second highest waterfall in Nigeria.
